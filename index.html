🛡️ Universal — Works for Every Situation
3 methods that work regardless of what you're feeling
▾
1
Box Breathing (90 seconds) — Inhale 4 · Hold 4 · Exhale 4 · Hold 4. Repeat 5 cycles. Fastest physiological reset available.
2
Name It to Tame It — Say the precise emotion aloud. "I feel disrespected" not "I feel bad." Naming reduces intensity by 30–50% within 90 seconds.
3
The One Question — Ask: "What is the one thing I can do right now within my control?" Write it. Do only that.
