🛡️ Universal — Works for Every Situation
3 methods that work regardless of what you're feeling
▾
1
Box Breathing (90 seconds) — Inhale 4 counts · Hold 4 · Exhale 4 · Hold 4. Repeat 4–5 cycles. This directly activates your parasympathetic nervous system and is the fastest physiological reset available regardless of the emotion. Do this before anything else.
2
Name It to Tame It — Say or write the precise emotion out loud: not "I feel bad" but "I feel disrespected" or "I feel afraid of failing." Neuroscience shows that labelling an emotion with specificity reduces its intensity by 30–50% within 90 seconds. Precision is the antidote to overwhelm.
3
The One Question — Ask yourself: "What is the one thing I can do right now that is within my control?" Write the answer in one sentence. Then do only that. This question cuts through every type of emotional state by returning focus to agency rather than circumstance.
